The Strand Arts Centre

152-154 Holywood Rd
Belfast
BT4 1NY

A jewel of art deco design, the Strand is the only remaining cinema in Northern Ireland from that golden era when, at one time, Belfast boasted over 40 picture palaces.
We have chosen this beautiful venue to celebrate our wedding as it represents us as well as any venue could. We both love Art Deco and "The Roaring Twenties." We love the style and romance of that era and of course we got engaged at a Great Gatsby show, so it felt right to continue the theme.
For more information on The Strand visit The Strand Arts Centre Website

Parisien

Cleaver House
56 Donegall Pl
Belfast
BT1 5GA

Feel the French vibe with Parisien, inside one of Belfast’s oldest Victorian buildings in Donegall Place. Parisien offers unrivalled views of the city and a chic Parisienne ambience. Adorned in elegant décor with carved dark woods and contrasting vibrant colours.
