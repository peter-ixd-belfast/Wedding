The Venues

The Strand Arts Centre

152-154 Holywood Rd
Belfast
BT4 1NY

Our love of the theatre and Art Deco style, matched with the ability to bring our two furry buddies made the Strand Cinema a no brainier. Combining the ability to be married on stage, with a relaxed environment is a full reflection of us as a couple.

For more information on The Strand visit The Strand Arts Centre Website
The Strand Arts Centre on Google Maps

Parisien

Cleaver House
56 Donegall Pl
Belfast
BT1 5GA

Parisien’s decor and colour pallete blew us away. The 1920s art deco inspired interior matched our vision for having a Gatsby style intimate gathering where our guests could feel transported to an opulent world with good food, strong drinks and wonderful company.

For more information on Parisien visit Parisien's Website
